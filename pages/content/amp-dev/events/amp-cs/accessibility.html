AMP Contributor Summit Accessibility

The AMP Contributor Summit is an inclusive conference and wants to be accessible.

We make sure that the AMP Contributor Summit is well accessible to people with physical disabilities, by both checking with the venue owner and by visiting them personally beforehand.

However, we are aware that accessibility issues are diverse and this does not cover everything.

If you are interested in our conference, but have any kind of accessibility problem that needs to be resolved before you can attend, please get in contact.

If you are in need of an assistant, they will not be required to request a ticket.

We would love to welcome you to AMP Contributor Summit!
